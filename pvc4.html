What we do

live_help

Private Vegetarian Catering is predominantly a food service that provides vegan and vegetarian curries on a weekly basis. All homemade and fresh, these tasty, authentic, wholesome dishes are a great way of incorporating a meat and dairy free dish into your weekly diet. Very reasonably priced, portions are enough for two and delivered on the day of your choice.

Food

thumb_up

All food is made using fresh ingredients (except where stated) and home ground spices. All food is vegan except the paneer dishes. (Tofu versions available). Local and UK produce is used where possible. Only Coconut Oil is used to fry the hard spices. Vegetarian versions contain organic butter. Only sea salt or Himalayan Pink Salt is used. No GM ingredients are used.

Ordering

announcement

A selection from 10 dishes rotating at 2 per week. All priced at £6 each. 2 for £10 every week. (Prices increased for orders outside Wolverhampton) Chapatti’s available @ 4 for £1. Gluten Free available. Rice or naan is not supplied. Small parties and events catered for, please contact us and we can discuss your requirements.

  • WEEK 1
  • Mung bean dahl or Paneer/Tofu, mushroom & spinach.
  • WEEK 2
  • Chickpeas and Spinach curry or Mexican chilli (made with sweet potato).
  • WEEK 3
  • Urid bean dahl or Cauliflower & Peas.
  • WEEK 4
  • Butternut squash curry or Chilli Paneer/Tofu.
  • WEEK 5
  • Green Beans & Peas or Okra & Potatoes.

ALL CURRIES ARE GLUTEN FREE. HOWEVER SOME OF THE SPICES USED HAVE PACKAGING THAT CONTAINS THE FOLLOWING STATEMENT: "MADE IN A PLANT THAT HANDLES NUTS, SESAME, GLUTEN, WHEAT, SOYA & MUSTARD."

The portions are served in 750ml tubs. As is normal in Indian home cooking, specific measurements are not used so the below measures are a rough guide of what each tub contains. Please click or tap on the dishes below for a list of thier ingredients

A text is sent out on Friday evenings to confirm what is being cooked in the forthcoming week.

Once a response has been received a delivery day will be arranged. When you receive the food you have the option to consume it or store it for a few days (it will keep in the fridge for up to 5 days).


Please reheat it thoroughly before consuming. Alternatively, all dishes and the chapatti’s can be frozen. The containers I am supplying can be frozen and are microwavable please return, re use or recycle them!

Cash, bank transfer or Paypal (+50p per transaction) payments accepted. Please contact me for details if you want to pay by transfer or Paypal.

About me

assignment_ind

My aim is to provide fresh, homemade, authentic, tasty and affordable food whilst maintaining the ethos of eliminating food waste. To date this business has not had any, this is continually implemented by only ever purchasing what is required, vegetable peelings are composted and all packaging is recycled.
